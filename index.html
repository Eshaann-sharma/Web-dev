My Top 5 Video Games

About Me

Hello! I'm Eshaan, known by my gamertag "Shen". I mostly play FPS games like Valorant and CS, as well as story-driven games. Here are my top 5 favorite video games:

Elden Ring

Elden Ring is an action RPG developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Set in an expansive world full of challenging enemies and epic boss battles, it offers a deep and immersive experience.

Hollow Knight

Hollow Knight is a critically acclaimed Metroidvania title developed by Team Cherry. Set in the hauntingly beautiful, insect-infested world of Hallownest, it features challenging gameplay, tight controls, and a richly atmospheric environment.

Zelda: Breath of the Wild

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an open-world action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo. It reinvents the Zelda series with a vast, open world to explore, filled with puzzles, quests, and stunning visuals.

Doom

Doom is a first-person shooter series developed by id Software. Known for its fast-paced, brutal gameplay and iconic weapons, Doom offers a high-octane experience fighting demons in a hellish setting.

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077, developed by CD Projekt Red, is an open-world RPG set in the dystopian future of Night City. It features a branching storyline, a vibrant world, and deep customization options for characters and gameplay.

Other Games I Enjoy

While these five are my top favorites, I also enjoy playing games like The Witcher 3, Dark Souls series, and Apex Legends. Each of these games offers something unique and engaging, from deep narratives to competitive gameplay.
